a little about us

We exist to help people bring their gifts into the world.

At our core, we care most about human dignity — and awakening it in others.

Drex is a place to be fully yourself and fully among others at the same time. People gather in small circles around their craft, contribute photos and reflections, and an issue becomes a real zine. Giving and receiving at once — nobody performing, nobody glossed over.

You belong here because you showed up, and that's enough. Communion over elitism. Come as you are; leave more yourself.
