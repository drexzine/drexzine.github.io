a little about us

We exist to help people bring their gifts into the world.

At our core, we care most about bringing people in communion with one another.

In Drex Circles, you'll gather with a consistent group to work on your craft. You'll contribute photos and reflections along the way, and those contributions become a fun & scrappy zine you can browse and share.

It works the way people have always gotten good at something: not from a teacher at the front of the room, but by making real things alongside others, at every level at once. There's a name for it, situated learning, and it's what Circles are built on.
