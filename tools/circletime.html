M1

The Clock

The object IS the concept. It's called Circle Time, and it's "the hour" — so draw the hour, and quarter it into the four things that happen inside it. Hover a wedge and it fills. Skeuomorphic in the way the payphone and the bell are: a thing you recognise instantly, doing a job.

what circle time is actually for

The hour you get people.

Not a work session. One hour a week where you can reach into somebody else's hands.

a DJ circle spends it going “how did you cross‑fade that?” — and someone shows them.

M2

The Table

Top‑down. A round table, four people, each doing one of the four things — one making, one watching, one asking, one being watched. It is simultaneously the four mechanisms and a literal circle of people. The most Drex object of the three: it's the circle, drawn.

M3

The Comic

Chielo's own example, drawn. Two panels: you're stuck; you ask; someone shows you their hands. This doesn't define the four mechanisms — it demonstrates one, and lets the four words sit underneath as a caption. The most cartoon of the three, and the least abstract.
