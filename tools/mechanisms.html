The four mechanisms

Chielo: Circle Time is not "when you do the challenge" — it's the hour you have access to people, through four mechanisms. Four icons, four words, one line each. It replaces the vague "practice together" node and it is the academic spine of the whole product, stated without jargon.

what circle time is actually for

The hour you get people.

Not a work session. It's the one hour a week you can reach into somebody else's hands.

Practisemake the thing
Observewatch their hands

Be observedlet them watch you

Some circles spend the hour making. A DJ circle spends it going “how did you cross-fade that?” — and someone shows them. Both are the same four things.
